SportsUp - Capstone Project

Project Overview

**SportsUp** is a mobile platform designed to connect players and sports watchers. Our system consists of two main modules:

  • Player System: Free users can find local matches and pitches, while premium users access advanced filters and chat features.
  • Watcher System: Free users discover match streaming locations, and premium users can reserve seating and receive early match notifications.

Additional revenue is generated through in-app advertising, affiliate sales, and partnerships with local businesses (e.g., pubs, restaurants). The app also supports event organization for soccer tournaments.
